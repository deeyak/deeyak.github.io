Visualization

What is Visualization?

Visualization is mentally walking through a competition in order to reduce anxiety and improve performance. You create a script in your head for how you want the competition to go. Fun fact, Michael Phelps used visualization in the olympics!

The Process

Step 1

Find a place free from distraction and get in a comfortable position, either sitting up or lying down.

Step 2

Close your eyes and take deep breaths

Step 3

Create a scene, go into detail and emotion

  • Create a scene of past successes
  • Imagine the environment, what you are wearing, what you see, what you feel, what you hear ect.
  • Imagine yourself performing well
  • Imagine the emotions you want to feel
  • Imagine a place that is relaxing to you such as the beach and take deep breaths

Visualization Scripts

Scripts for different sports to follow

Close your eyes and take a deep breath in through your nose and out through your mouth. Picture yourself stepping onto the soccer field. Feel the grass beneath your cleats and hear the familiar sounds around you—teammates talking, whistles blowing, the crowd in the distance. You feel prepared. Your body feels loose, strong, and ready.

As the game begins, imagine yourself moving confidently. You stay calm and focused. The ball comes to your feet and your first touch is controlled and smooth. You look up and clearly see the field around you. You make sharp passes, communicate with teammates, and trust your instincts. Your movements feel natural.

Picture yourself staying composed even during pressure moments. Instead of worrying about mistakes, you remain present. You recover quickly and refocus. Visualize making the play you want—a strong pass, defensive stop, assist, or goal. See yourself executing it successfully.

Notice how confident and relaxed you feel. Your breathing stays steady. You trust your preparation and training. You do not need to be perfect. You simply play your game.

Take one final deep breath and remind yourself: I am prepared, focused, and ready.
